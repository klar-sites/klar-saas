Features

More than just a blank page.

Mosaic blends the freedom of traditional journaling with the power of private AI to help you connect the dots.

End-to-end Encryption

Your thoughts are yours alone. We use industry-leading encryption so not even our servers can read your entries.

AI Pattern Recognition

Discover hidden trends in your mood and productivity. Our AI gently points out correlations you might miss.

Smart Prompts

Staring at a blank page? Mosaic suggests context-aware prompts based on your previous entries and current time of day.

Audio Journaling

Prefer to speak? Record your thoughts with high-fidelity audio that transcribes automatically and analyzes sentiment.

Mood Tracking

Tag your entries with custom moods and see beautiful, interactive charts of your emotional journey over time.

Cross-Device Sync

Start an entry on your phone during the commute and finish it on your tablet at home. Everything syncs seamlessly.
